About Us

Why We Started

In many rural and underprivileged areas, access to clean drinking water is still a distant dream. Children often resort to unsafe sources like puddles, drains, or contaminated tanks—risking diseases and long-term health issues.

Seeing this harsh reality inspired us to create Pure Flavour—a simple yet effective solution to make clean water accessible, portable, and affordable for all.

Our goal is not just to provide clean water, but to restore hope, dignity, and health to communities that need it most.

Our Solution

Pure Flavour : Your Pocket-Friendly Water Purifier

Pure Flavour is a compact, pocket-friendly water filter made for daily use by anyone, anywhere—especially students, workers, and families in underserved communities.

Using activated charcoal, cotton filtration, and nano-mesh technology, it removes dirt, germs, and foul odors—making even muddy water safe to drink in seconds.

Lightweight, affordable, and easy to carry, Pure Flavour gives people the power to drink clean water wherever they are—at school, at work, or on the move.

Steps to Use

Step 1

Take out the Pure Flavour Cartridge Filter from its packet.

Step 2

Open the sealed wrap around the perforation of the cartridge and open its sealed hooked mouth.

Step 3

Open your drinking bottle and around the mouth of it place and attach the filter tightly. Lock it unless the ring with the string sits properly.

Step 4

Entangle the bottle and pour water through the cartridge and see how the dirt sticks in there.
